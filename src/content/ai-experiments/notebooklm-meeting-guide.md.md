# 🎙️ Meeting Insights with NotebookLM 

*In just 5 min get amazing insights from long, boring or complicated meeting. Become instant guru with this practical tutorial * 

👥 **Who:** Professionals | ⏱️ **Time:** 5 - 10  min

**What do you need**
- 🎼 Recording on the phone
- 📒 NotebookLM 

👨‍🍳 **Steps**
1. Record the meeting on your phone 
2. Hit share button and choose NotebookLM App 
3. Once source uploaded go to Studio 
4. Choose: Slides + Describle: Simple, Clear, Sticky figures 

💡 **Tip:** Simple an clear visualization will give you great overview and visual insights.
